What is Signon?


"Signon Translations" is a complete language translator where we can translate any mode of communication into another mode from any language to another. This will help people to communicate with anyone without any barrier of language or accent. As this can translate sign languages , deaf and mute people can communicate or share ideas with others through hand gestures. There are basically three modes of communications and they are :

Text

Voice

Hand Gestures
